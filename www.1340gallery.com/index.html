  • 1340FOUNDATION
    MADaboutART
    Each quarter,1340Foundation features a different art-related cause. For Q3 we proudly feature MADaboutART. This non-profit organization was founded in 2001 to fight HIV using art and education.

    Pioneered in South Africa, its award-winning curriculum for children and youth uses art as the platform to ignite imagination, have fun and achieve social change.

    EMERGING ARTISTS
    As a platform built by artists, we at 1340Foundation are passionate about empowering emerging artists. We are dedicated to promoting these artists and we aid in supporting their development, as well as various international non-profit organizations whose focus is on creating opportunities for promising talent in a variety of artistic and cultural pursuits.

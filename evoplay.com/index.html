About Us

EvoPlay — leading developer of integrated products and solutions for the online gaming industry. Based on many years of experience in the segment of online entertainment, we have designed and developed a set of products designed for the creation and effective management of gaming systems. Thanks to our b2b solutions and technologies, hundreds of companies around the world enable millions of players to satisfy their passion.

The EvoPlay Company was founded in 2003 by a group of serial entrepreneurs who had successful experience in the online gaming industry. After the release of several b2b solutions, which received wide popularity, the company expanded its activities beyond the production of games and gaming platforms. In a series of mergers and alliances, now EvoPlay is an international company, experts of which developed leading solutions in the fields of: gambling mathematics, games and gaming platforms, multi-platform CMS, payment providers, users support and retain, analytics and finance, recruitment and traffic management.

UniqueDoc

Platform for secure communication between healthcare professionals and help-seekers

UniqueDoc is a secure and confidential communication platform for private healthcare consultations, medical business conferences, and networking events. It offers messaging and files-sharing together with managing and scheduling appointments.

UniqueDoc main features
Profile
Personal Profile

Doctors and patients can create their custom profiles to connect with colleagues and other specialists. Here they can add their professional skills, courses, place of work or any additional information of any importance.

News Feed
News Feed

Here users can check out the latest healthcare-related news, get information about the recent events, post news, photos, videos, and anything related to their interest of field of expertise.

Messaging
Messaging

A messenger-like interface provides a familiar, convenient, and multifunctional experience of message exchange. Users can attach files, send images, documents, and even audio files. In addition, the chatting history gets saved and can be easily searched later.

Dashboard
Dashboard

Users can search for, join-in or check out the latest posts in public or private groups with healthcare professionals to receive new information or consultations.

Event Calendar
Event calendar

The calendar offers users to manage their events, set up scheduled appointments, create new gatherings, and check out reminders or the address of the place to visit. In addition. they can invite other users to the events of any kind, be it a conference or a personal visit to doctor.

Developing the necessities
Technologies we applied

We have developed a platform-independent application for instant or non-urgent communication between healthcare providers and patients seeking professional help. We have used the latest developing tools to ensure the highest level of protection and security for the passed information, provide the seamless message-exchange experience, and cover the all-format-supported file sharing system to eliminate the need in third-party apps.

Data security

Used RNCryptor and SSKeyChain for data encryption and user verification.

Scaling

Applied AFNetwork to put down a foundation for increasing number of users and requests.

File sharing

PDFNet, GPU Image, and SDWebImage for fast files procession.

Chat

SockerRocket and OpenTok cover the real-time user-server interactions

What we achieved
Summary

Understanding the specifics of the industry, our team worked on providing the highest level of security and data confidentiality with a several-steps verification process and personal verification. With UniqueDoc users can chat, call, and share files, being assured they are protected by safety protocols and double-encrypted secure data storage.

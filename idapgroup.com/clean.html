Clean: Energy Tracking application

The way we are living our lives severely affects the Earth’s biosphere. As a step towards the popularization of eco-energy use, we collaborated with an American energy supplier in creating an app that would help people get electricity from green sources and track its consumption.

About client

Smart Energy Company is a US-based electricity provider. This company supplies energy only from renewable sources like wind and sun thereby saving the environment and fighting climate change.

Main Tasks

The app had to make the Smart Energy services more accessible and easy-to-use for existing customers, attract new ones by the referral program, and help in gathering an eco-friendly community. The client has developed a rewarding system and wanted the app to become a platform that would let users collect rewards and track energy consumption.

How Users Benefit from Clean?

Our team has developed CLEAN Energy App — the ultimate tool for power consumption tracking and popularization of green electricity. It rewards people with real money to pay for electricity and discounts to save on energy.

about referral system

CLEAN referral program helps to assemble the community of eco-oriented people and reward those who truly care for the environment as much as Smart Energy does.

User can follow up the activity of his referrals and share his green energy experience.

Users who invite friends get points convertible into real money for paying electricity bills.

All in one Screen

CLEAN is the best-value solution for green electricity consumers and those who prefer renewable energy over fossil fuel. Our team integrated gamification into the app to tie up the rewarding system with fun user experience.

Multiple gamification elements within the app stimulate users to be more rational with energy consumption.

With CLEAN users get real money for their concern over ecology.

Depending on how many points users have, they can exchange them for discounts and pay cheaper electricity bills.

Each badge reflects the number of points user has collected and motivates him to collect further.

To give back to people who opted for renewable energy, the client launches prize draws weekly.

Going Green is Easy and Fun

CLEAN is an app that eases the transition from fossil fuels to green energy. Our team succeeded in creating a top-notch mobile app facilitating people to go green and take care of the environment.

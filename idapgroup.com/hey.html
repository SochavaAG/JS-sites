How heyyy! works

  • Personal Profile

    User can sign up either with their Facebook account or by creating a profile using a phone number. To make your profile more popular, the users should upload photos.

    The more photos a user has, the more popular the profile will be.

  • Search Filters

    Users can use 3 different filters (sex, location, and age) to narrow the search.

  • Browse and choose a partner

    App displays possible matches according to the set filters. The user can tap on a heart to like a person, or X to skip and view other profiles.

  • It’s a match!

    When both users have a match, they can chat.

Main features

  • Inbuilt chat

    Google Channel based IRC eliminates the risks of lengthy time gaps during conversations.

  • Secure platfrom

    Users can message and share their data only with the matches they approve.

  • Integration with social networks

    Facebook SDK provides for quick authorisation and media sharing.

  • Engaging animations

    Micro interactions keep user’s attention on the app and enhance their in-app experience.

Summary

Heyyy! is a smooth and convenient dating app for IOS platform. Our team has worked closely with the client to develop and deliver the concept they had in mind.

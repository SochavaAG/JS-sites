eCola is a business-to-business mobile ordering platform with salesforce integration built to control ordering system and simplify the communication between the bottler and beverage distributors.

MissionApplication had to:

Analyse trends that occur in company’s sales volume

Provide information on products and their nutrition data

Simplify the process of ordering beverages and other merchandise

Offer the best deals based on distributors preferences

AboutWhat is eCola?

In short, eCola is a B2B mobile application designed for retail stores, restaurants and food service distributors.

CompletedChallenges
Real time Inventory

We had to connect data storage that would update in real-time as soon as some changes in the inventory were made, or even if one stock item was moved from one category to another.

Integration with Cola’s Salesforce CRM

The application had to seamlessly integrate with company’s Salesforce system without causing any significant changes to the back-end infrastructure.

Secure payments

Connecting a safe payment gateway that would comply with all Payment Card Industry standards and safely store user payment information.

StructureHow does it work?

It helps distributors order lacking production and receive additional advertising materials for a proper arrangement of merchant tables.

Home screen

By opening the app users go to the main screen with a list of products.

They can filter the bevarages by type, popularity, volume and select their favorites.

Product Page

Users can revise product's characteristics, change quantity and get the final price.

Shopping cart

Shopping cart allows looking through the final order and fill in delivery details.

Order Status & History

Ordering history allows to see what you ordered, check details, current status and change the delivery information if needed. Also you'd be able to check what you ordered last time and automatically re-order the same items.

EcolaSummary

The primary purpose of the eCola is to increase order frequency and boost revenues by giving distributors a valuable and appealing mobile tool.

Our team has managed to create a full-scale mobile application that achieved all set goals and met the expectations of our clients regarding total automation of their inter-communication with distributors. Despite a very demanding time frame, we worked extra-fast and finished on time.
